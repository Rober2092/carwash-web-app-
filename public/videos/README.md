# Video Upload Instructions

## How to Add Your Video

1. **Get your video file** from your phone/computer
2. **Rename it to:** `detailing-video.mp4`
3. **Copy it to this folder:** `/public/videos/`

## Supported Formats
- MP4 (recommended)
- WebM

## File Size
- Try to keep videos under 50MB for best performance
- You can compress videos using online tools if needed

## Once Uploaded
- Refresh your browser
- The video will automatically appear in the "See Us In Action" section
- It will have full video controls (play, pause, volume, etc.)

---

**Note:** The video will show a placeholder until you upload your file.
